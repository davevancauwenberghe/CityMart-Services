Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

CityMart Services operates as a Discord bot for the CityMart Group and may interact with external services to provide features.

Data Handling

  • No personal data is permanently stored by the bot.
  • Messages sent to the bot are processed in-memory and may be relayed to third-party APIs for generating responses.
  • hallAI requests are processed via the OpenAI API, using a Cloudflare Worker as a proxy.
  • Discord messages and commands are processed using the Discord API.

Your Rights

If you wish to have all conversation data associated with your session removed, please contact the server administrator or use any provided in-bot command for data deletion (if available).
